Questions: 

  1. Which companies are the top 5 Applicants of H1B Visa between 2011- 2016 ? Are all of them Outsourcing companies ?
  2. Which Product Companies are top H1B Applicants between 2011- 2016 ? How comparable they are with the top applicants (Outsourcing companies) ?
  3. Is there any common trend ?
  4. In 2016, how the median wage differs between the Outsourcing companies and Product Companies ?

Are the chart types (e.g., bar, line, scatterplot) appropriately chosen with respect to the kind of data shown (e.g., categorical vs. quantitative, continuous vs. discrete)?

    • The first & second charts are Grouped Bar chart where the height of the bar indicates the number of H1B application (quantitative) filed by each company for 6 different years (categorical).

    • The Box plot displays the median wage of 2016 applications for each complany. The Box Plot is choosen for showing distribution of wage data.

Is the data shown in the context of an overview?

  • The first chart shows that the Outsourcing companies are the highest applicants of H1B Visa for past 6 years ( 2011 - 1016 ). [Question 1]
  • The 2nd chart shows the name of the Product companies with highest number of H1B Application for past 6 years ( 2011 - 1016 ). [Question 2 - part 1]

  • The 2nd chart initially has the y-axis range same as the 1st chart, so that the data can be visually compared. We can see here that the number of H1B applications by the Product companies are much lesser than the Outsourcing Companies.[Question 2 - part 2]

  • The 3rd Chart shows the wage distributions of all 10 companies in 2016. 
  • The Outsourcing companies in blue and Product Companies are in Red color.
  • There is a line in blue, indicating the median wage of all Outsourcing companies and another line in red, indicating the median wage of all Product Companies.
